# ProtectYaNeck Beta Installation Guide

Welcome to the ProtectYaNeck beta program! This guide will help you install and test the extension.

## Prerequisites

- Google Chrome browser (version 88 or higher)
- About 5 minutes of your time

## Installation Steps

### Step 1: Download the Extension

1. Download the extension package from [provided link or get files from `/dist` folder]
2. If you received a `.zip` file, extract it to a folder on your computer

### Step 2: Enable Developer Mode in Chrome

1. Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions`
2. In the top-right corner, toggle **Developer mode** ON

![Developer Mode Toggle](https://developer.chrome.com/static/docs/extensions/mv3/getstarted/development-basics/image/extensions-page-e702561e3bf14.png)

### Step 3: Load the Extension

1. Click the **Load unpacked** button in the top-left
2. Navigate to and select the `dist` folder (or extracted folder)
3. Click **Select Folder**

### Step 4: Verify Installation

1. You should see "ProtectYaNeck" in your extensions list
2. The extension icon (shield) should appear in your Chrome toolbar
3. Click the puzzle piece icon and pin ProtectYaNeck for easy access

## How to Use

### Automatic Scanning
- Visit any website with Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, or sign-up agreements
- ProtectYaNeck will automatically detect and analyze the page
- A scanning indicator will appear in the bottom-right corner

### Manual Scanning
1. Click the ProtectYaNeck icon in your toolbar
2. Click the "Scan This Page" button
3. View the results in the overlay that appears

## Providing Feedback

Your feedback is crucial for improving ProtectYaNeck!

### In-Extension Feedback
1. Click the extension icon
2. Use the feedback form at the bottom
3. Select a category and describe your experience

### Bug Reports
When reporting bugs, please include:

### Feature Requests
We love hearing your ideas! Use the feedback form or email us at feedback@protectyaneck.com

## Troubleshooting

### Extension Not Detecting Pages

1. Refresh the page you're viewing
2. Check if the site is in your whitelist (Settings > Whitelist)
3. Try disabling and re-enabling the extension

### Scan Results Not Appearing

1. Wait a few seconds - analysis can take time for long documents
2. Check your internet connection
3. Try clicking "Scan This Page" manually in the popup

### Usage Counter Shows Wrong Number

1. Open Settings > General
2. Check your current period dates
3. Usage resets on the 1st of each month

### Extension Icon Not Visible

1. Click the puzzle piece icon in Chrome toolbar
2. Find ProtectYaNeck and click the pin icon

## Known Issues (Beta)

- PDF analysis is not yet supported
- Some dynamically-loaded agreements may not be detected immediately
- Very long documents (50,000+ words) may timeout

## Privacy & Data

- All analysis happens locally in your browser
- We do not store the content of agreements you scan
- Usage data is stored locally in Chrome
- Lead submissions (lawyer referrals) are sent to our secure servers

## Getting Help

- Email: support@protectyaneck.com
- Response time: Within 24-48 hours

## Uninstallation

1. Go to `chrome://extensions`
2. Find ProtectYaNeck
3. Click **Remove**
4. Confirm removal
